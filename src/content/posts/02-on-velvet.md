The hardest part wasn't the velvet. The velvet was three radial gradients, a film grain layer, and a faint inset highlight on every card. The hardest part was making the velvet recede when there's writing to read. Glow that helps the type instead of competing with it. I think we got there. The first essay landed without the page fighting me, which is the only test that matters.
